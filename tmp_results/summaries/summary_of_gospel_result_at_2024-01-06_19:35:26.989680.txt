 The short exposition of the Gospel is a summary of a large work that exists in manuscript and cannot be published in Russia . This work may be in the public domain in countries with longer native copyright terms, such as the United States, where copyright protection lasts for the life of the author plus 70 years .
 In my treatment of the Gospel of John there are no transpositions, but everything follows the order of the verses in the Gospels . In making the Gospel into twelve chapters came about of itself from the sense of the teaching, which is the central theme .
 The true life is life in the present, common to all men, manifesting itself in love . True life unites with the Father, the source and foundation of life . The first two chapters of the text are related as cause and effect .
 The Lord's Prayer is a short and concise expression of the whole teaching of Jesus . It is not a full exposition, but rather a summary of the Gospels in the third part . Each phrase of the prayer corresponds to the meaning and sequence of the chapters . The full exposition includes all the teachings of Jesus, including the Sermon on the Mount and Parable of the Good Samaritan .
